About Micro-Work

Micro-Work has a small team of female medical professionals and provides product regulatory services governed by the Taiwan Food and Drug Administration (TFDA). Founder Song, Shunlyn started with a one-man studio when he was 37 years old, and within five years the number of employees increased to three. Ten years later, it was officially registered as a legal person (8 employees). Twenty years later, Senior Staff spun off as a spin-off. Parent company We have a small and beautiful office in order to provide exquisite services to our customers.

The company's working hours are flexible, employees can choose their own work location, and colleagues support each other, serving the regulatory business of the international and domestic food, cosmetics, medical equipment, and pharmaceutical industries. To operate a drug regulatory service business, professionalism is the basic condition, and teamwork, mutual support, and quick response are the characteristics of micro workers.

Founding philosophy

Song, Shunlyn understood the situation of women working in the technology field who "cannot express themselves" after marriage, and further thought about how women can balance work and family.

In 1997, Song Shunlyn left her job at a law firm. Then she started to assist in individual legal affairs and established her own studio. Later, through introductions and recommendations from customers, the studio's business volume grew steadily. Song Shunlyn gradually recruited a group of women with scientific and technological backgrounds, allowing them to use their talents and have a stable income to support these women in their families and businesses. Achieving self-integration and self-management in career, and practice a life of focusing on family and career.

Five core business concepts:

  • Concern
  • Transparency
  • Partnership
  • Work-Life Balance
  • Empowerment
